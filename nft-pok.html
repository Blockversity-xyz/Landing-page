your Community:

Wallet to Wallet
Instant Payment
With NFTs, transactions are wallet to wallet, so your receive your payment instantly.  

Buyers can pay with tokens or a credit card. Reward them for using tokens, and waive the credit card fee.

The key to Unlock Access
to Your Content:
Before unlokcing access to your content the system validates the presence of your NFT course  in the user’s wallet; a much better protection than a login/password that can be easily shared.
